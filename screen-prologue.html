Welcome to The Bakery

Heroes come through here often. It’s your job to bake something delicious to will satisfy their taste buds and buff their stats!

There are many ingredients to choose from. Each one has its own unique magical buffing properties. It’s up to you to decide which ingredients you think will create the best buffed baked good. Then serve it up and set that hero on their path to glory!

Your baking could make a huge difference in their battles ahead so listen closely to what each hero has to say. It’s important work. But it’s also good for business.
